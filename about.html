ABOUT ALICE CONTRACTORS

Learn more about us

Established in Zimbabwe in 1997, Alice Contractors has grown from a small family-run company, to a nationally recognised building contractor. It is registered under Category “A” of the Zimbabwe Building Contractors Association (ZBCA)...

Alice Contractors directly employs a robust team of 15 permanent staff and over 50 contract artisans. We have built strong relationships with a host of professional partner companies to complement our skills.

VISION

our vision

Our vision is to become the contractor of first choice in Zimbabwe. We would like to have a name synonymous with building construction even to the average Zimbabwean.

MISSION

our mission

Alice Contractors is dedicated to providing quality construction, technical and management services to our customers.

We will endeavor to provide superior finishes and services, meeting international standards by constantly revising training programmes for our personnel, keeping abreast with latest technology and upholding national laws.

We will aim to be the preferred contractor with all our past and current clients for any future works they may have. Through referrals and recommendations, our excellence will be well known on a broader level

We will foster close communication between directors, senior management and the workforce which we believe is vital in maintaining a cohesive and enthusiastic approach.

EFFECTIVE TEAMWORK

we support teamwork spirit

The key objectives are to improve performance through increased productivity and competitiveness; to improve the industry’s image by changing its culture, developing its people and engaging better with communities and customers; and to engage and take action with individuals, businesses, organisations and industry associations.
